Bookings

To book one of our beautiful models, or any enquiry you may have please fill out the booking form below that will reach us directly from the moment you send it.

Please do not hesitate to contact us by telephone for a quicker response on 07999390999.

Hope to hear from you soon... 

All information and data submitted through our booking form is secure and encrypted for your own privacy and protection. We are fully compliant with the data protection act and all information are never shared with third parties and is only for the sole use of management. The same also applies to telephone enquiries and bookings.

Please remember this is only a request form, for a possible booking with one of our escorts. Filling in this form does not ensure that your booking is confirmed until we have checked the availability of your chosen model.

We advise you to enter as much information as possible regarding how you would like to be contacted or if there is a specific time and date. Please enter this information into the special instructions box at the bottom of the booking form.

Once we have confirmed the availability of your chosen elite model, we will then contact you to re-confirm the booking time and date.

In the event that your chosen model not being available, we will endeavour to do our best to find the best suitable match by looking closely at your specifications that you have requested.
